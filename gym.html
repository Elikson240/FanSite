Jump to information about:Gym Leaders

Making a return after being absent in Pokemon Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun, and Ultra Moon are the Pokemon Gyms. And for the first time ever the two games, Sword and Shield, have version-exclusive gym leaders. Before you challenge the gym leaders you will now need to complete gym missions before proceeding. When participating in the gym challenge you will have to complete all gym missions and defeat all eight gym leaders then win the final tournament at the end of the game. After that you will be able to fight the champion and take the crown.

Turfield Gym

Grass type gym leader will have Gossifluer(Lv 19) This is a Grass type and is weak to Fire, Flying, Ice, Poison, and Bug. Eldegoss(Lv 20) is a grass type and is weak to Flying, Ice, Poison, and Bug.

Hulbury Gym

Water type gym leader will have Goldeen(Lv 20) a water typ weak to Electric and Grass. Arrokuda(Lv 23) and is also weak to Electric and Grass. Drednaw(Lv 24) a water and rock type that is weak to Electric, Grass, Fighting, and Ground.

Motostoke Gym

Fire type gym leader will have Ninetales(Lv 25) which is weak to water, rock, and ground. Arcanine(Lv 25) is weak to water, rock, and ground. Centiskorch(Lv 27) is weak to water, rock, and ground.

Stow-on-Side Gym

Ghost type gym leader will have Galarian Yamask(Lv 34) which is weak to dark, steel, and water. Galarian Corsola is weak to dark and ghost. Mimikyu(Lv 34) is weak to ghost and steel. Gengar(Lv 36) is weak to ghost, dark, and physic.
