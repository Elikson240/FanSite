Pokemon Sword and Shield are 2019 role-playing video games developed by Game Freak and published by The Pokemon Company and Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch and take place in the Galar region. New feature is the Pokemon Camp which allows players to interact and play with their Pokemon and cook various types of curry to provide bonuses for Pokemon and heal them.
