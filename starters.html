Reference Gym Challenge Dynamax Home

Before setting off to embark on a journey, the player along with their rival, Hop, receive one of three starter Pokemon: Sobble, Scorbunny, and Grookey from Hop's older brother, Leon, the Galar region Champion. From these three Pokemon, the best choice would be the super cute and energetic fire type, Scorbunny. This starter is the best choice early game because it will a type advantage over the first gym which specializes in grass-types. This is my opinion to start off the game with an advantage, but you can choose any starter that appeals the most to you.
